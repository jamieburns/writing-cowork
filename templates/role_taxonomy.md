## Naming Notes

- All role names lowercase, no spaces, use `-` for multi-word names (e.g., `sub-editor`)
- Combinations use `+` to join (e.g., `pm+writer`)
- Case-sensitive in task assignment
- If you rename a standard role locally, document the mapping here (e.g., "we call 'analysis' 'research'")
